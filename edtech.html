Goal

Create a minimum viable edtech product to develop soft skills in low and middle-low socio-economic sectors. The main objectives were to identify:

  • The market niche, know their needs, and understand the variables to evaluate that demonstrate an impact on the person.
  • The pedagogy and products to make a massive delivery.

Problem

Millions of young people lack character and soft skills that inhibit their economic and professional development.

Metodology

User research (Qualitative):

  • Conducted 20+ deep interviews to 3 possible targets to understand their needs and motivation among others.
  • Analyzed the information provided to identify drivers, insights, considerations and open questions to create the product.
  • Created a buyer persona for the three possible targets (university directors, HR corporate leaders and students) to understand their profile, life goals, motivations, and fears.

User research (Quantitative analysis):

  • Understood the global trends, market, and competitors around developing skills for the future.
  • Analyzed 20+ products and services that resolve the needs with similar business models and/or methodologies.

Prototyping and Testing:

  • Designed the MVP to validate the hypothesis of the solution with the main features of the product.
